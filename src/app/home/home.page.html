MANUFATURING

Technological Advantage & Superior Products

Megha Healthcare (MHL) has collaborated with manufacturing companies who specialise in manufacturing formulations of various dosage forms and whose facilities are cGMP compliant and approved globally. MHL ensures production of quality medicines and implements technologies to deliver superior products. The plants have the capacity to produce solutions from the start to finish right from raw material processing to packaging. An all inclusive and comprehensive product quality is promised and is tried and tested at different stages. MHL since its inception has been constantly striving to provide technological superiority, high quality products at best competitive prices.

QUALITY FORMULATIONS

State-of-the-Art Facilities

GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) Standards followed by MHL include WHO norms laid down by the World Health Organisation (WHO) as well as cGMP norms laid down in the Revised Schedule M by the Drug Controller (General) India, Ministry of Health, Government of India. The testing of products is followed as per (WHO) TRS guidelines. Our manufacturing companies' operations are ISO 9001:2000 certified. Our packaging materials confirm to International standards. MHL manufactures over 64 formulations in different dosage forms and are regional leaders in the Anti fungal (Dermatology) range with a steadily growing portfolio. We have a strong team of 150 + sales and marketing professionals who cater to more than 30,000 doctors across just one state. Our finished formulations are available at over 50,000 retail pharmacies with over 100 distributors in every corner of the state.
